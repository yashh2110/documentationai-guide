---
title: "Getting Started with Paperguide"
---

To start using **Paperguide**, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit our [sign-up page](https://paperguide.com/signup) and create an account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the Dashboard" icon="layout">
    Familiarize yourself with the dashboard and main features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Your Profile" icon="settings">
    Customize your profile settings to tailor the experience to your needs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
  Paperguide supports multiple languages. Adjust language settings in your profile.
</Callout>
