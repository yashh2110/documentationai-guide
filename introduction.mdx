## Getting Started with Paperguide

You use Paperguide to create, organize, and collaborate on project documentation in a centralized space. It provides tools for structuring content, integrating with version control, and sharing knowledge across teams. Start by setting up your first project to explore its features.

## Quick Setup Steps

Follow these steps to create your first documentation space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user">
    Sign up at paperguide.com with your email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="New Project" icon="plus">
    Click the `+` button and name your project, such as `My Docs`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Pages" icon="file">
    Create your first page using the editor, adding text and images.
  </Step>
</Steps>
