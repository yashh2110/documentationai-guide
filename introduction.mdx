Welcome to **Paperguide**, the AI-powered research platform designed to simplify the research experience for students, researchers, and academics. With a suite of integrated tools, Paperguide streamlines the research process from discovery to writing.

Paperguide offers:

- **Smart Discovery**: Leverage AI to find relevant research materials quickly.
- **Intelligent Analysis**: Gain deeper insights with AI-driven analysis tools.
- **Streamlined Writing**: Use the writing assistant to craft well-structured papers effortlessly.

Explore Paperguide's features to enhance productivity and save time.
